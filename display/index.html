Save time, learn more!

CLook saves the time wasted in scouring mutliple open courseware sites and numerous courses for choosing the best course, and gives you the best course in the discipline of your choice in a matter of seconds! Choose from courses from sites like Coursera, Udacity, Udemy and many more. Now you can utilize your time more efficiently to learn something new. CLook is open source, free to download and very easy to use. So start with the best MOOC now!

Features

Easy Lookup

Enter the preference of your discipline and let us find the best course for you. Further filter the courses based on universities if you want.

Ordered by Ratings

We choose the best course on the basis of ratings of users like you, who've taken the course before. We compare user ratings across multiple sites for best results.

Up to Date

If there's a course of your choice, by your favorite university, rest assured that we have it. We like to keep things up to date and fresh.

Recommend a Friend

Choose a course based on the number of friends who've taken the course before you. No personal recommendations required!
